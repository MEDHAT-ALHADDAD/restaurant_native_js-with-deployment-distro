About Us

Our History

Started in 0001, krusty krab quickly established itself as a culinary icon par excellence in Bikini Bottom. With its unique brand of world krab-burger that can be found nowhere else, it enjoys patronage from the A-list clientele in Bikini Bottom. Featuring four of the best three-star Michelin chefs in the world, you never know what will arrive on your plate the next time you visit us.

The restaurant traces its humble beginnings to The Frying Pan, a successful chain started by our CEO, Mr. Peter Pan, that featured for the first time the world's best cuisines in a pan.

Corporate Leadership

Eugene H. Chief Epicurious Officer

Our CEO, Eugene, credits his hardworking neverland immigrant parents who undertook the arduous journey to the shores of bikini bottom with the intention of giving their children the best future. His mother's wizardy in the kitchen whipping up the tastiest dishes with whatever is available inexpensively at the neighbors, was his first inspiration to create the Krusty Krab for which The Frying Pan became well known. He brings his zeal to this restaurant, pioneering cross-cultural culinary connections.

Katsuki Bakugou Chief Food Officer

Our CFO, Katsuki, as he is affectionately referred to by his colleagues, comes from a long established family tradition in fishing by boom and produce. His experiences growing up on a island in the east blue outback gave him great appreciation for varieties of food sources. As he puts it in his own words, Everything that boom, wins, and everything that stays, pays!

Luffy D. Monkey Chief Taste Officer

Blessed with the most discerning gustatory sense, luffy, our CTO, personally ensures that every dish that we serve meets his exacting tastes. Our chefs dread the tongue lashing that ensues if their dish does not meet his exacting standards. He lives by his motto, You click only if you survive my lick.

SpongeBob SquarePants Executive Chef

Award winning three-star Michelin chef with wide International experience having worked closely with whos-who in the culinary world, he specializes in creating mouthwatering krusty-burger. He says, Put together every thing , and you get a winning shit!
